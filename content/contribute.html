Contribute to the Judgmental.org.uk project!

No matter your background or skills, if you share our vision for open and accessible legal information then you can help us achieve it.

Here are some ways you can help:

  • Spread the word—we need more people to know about the problems with access to UK legal information and how we plan to solve them. The more people who know, the more people might want to help!

  • Tell us what you think—read about our purpose and goals, use the site, and let us know your thoughts by tweeting us @Judgmentals, or posting to our mailing list. We'd be particularly interested in hearing from people who aren't law or techie people like us, so we can know if we're on the right track! Feedback is the breakfast of champions, after all.

  • Help with accessibility—we're keen to make sure our site design excludes no-one, and we could really use some experienced input in that regard.

  • Help with design—with all the excitement of HTML5 and semantic technologies, it can be hard to make sure the site works well with older browsers and systems. We'd be more than happy to share this particular burden!

  • Lots more—click here to see our long-standing to-do list, ranging from site admin tasks to information architecture issues to ideas for new features.

    Please join the discussion on that site on an issue that takes your interest (you will have to sign up for a free account). Again, your skills background doesn't matter; everyone has something to contribute, even if just your opinions as a user (which are crucial, actually!) and we want all of you to be involved.
