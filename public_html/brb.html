We'll be back soon

We have received a libel threat for making an unedited court judgment, a document of public record, available to the public.

We are working with our hosting providers to have the matter resolved as soon as possible.

If you want to keep up to date with what's going on, or think you could help us out, you can get in touch via the mailing list or Tweeting @Judgmentals.

In the mean time, you can find every judgment hosted on this website and many more on the BAILII website, which is also free and open to the public.

We remain committed to enabling and promoting the study of UK law by making a comprehensive archive of material available to the public for easy access and use.

Thank you for your patience! Watch this space!
